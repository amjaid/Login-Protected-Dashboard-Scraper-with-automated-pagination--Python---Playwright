PROPOSAL TIPS
===============================
- Always customize greeting and first line to mention the client’s site/job.

- Ask 1 clarifying question—shows professionalism.  
- Offer deliverables clearly (CSV/JSON/Excel, logs, retries).  
- Maintain polite and confident tone, avoid overselling.
